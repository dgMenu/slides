Introduction

dgMenu is a digital menu system which aims to replace the traditional menus available in various restaurants, bars, cafes & other outlets. The purpose of this is known from the name itself and that is to digitalize the menu. Keeping in mind the amount of paper & other material goes into the use of the menu & the maintenance, our team came up with dgMenu. A step taken to modernize & Improvise for the betterment of the society.

So, this dgMenu will be basically available in restaurants, bars, cafes & various outlets. A QR Code will be present at the venue or at the table you're sitting at, so a user just needs to scan the code & the menu will be present on their phone. Users just need to go through the menu & order. Items in the menu are updated on a daily basis.

Keeping in mind the present situation i.e. the COVID - 19 pandemic, this method works effectively due to the restrictions that are imposed like keeping safe distance between people, avoiding touching things in public places & others. “Contact less” is the word the outlets can use to make the customers safe.

In the dgMenu, there are no hardships faced in making changes or updating the menu items. A simple UI is maintained, so that the restaurant/cafe owner or the I.T person in charge of the module, is able to find it simple & easier to work on.

Fast food stalls & other outlets are gonna gain from this module/system as their food inventory keeps on increasing with new dishes which ultimately will attract more & more customers & so, the workload of the menu will reduce due to dgMenu.

Need for the project

The main benefit dgMenu system provides is eliminating the use of paper completely. dgMenu system can benefit the GenZ restaurants and cafes in multiple ways, it will be quite time saving for busy restaurants as the menu will suggest “you eat what you see”, so whenever the waiter is up for the order you already know what to eat.

It will be an add on advantage for the people who prefer contact less menus especially in times of COVID19 wherein the customer will only have to scan the menu without actually getting in contact with the paper menu.

Problem Statement

As we know that many of the restaurants in Goa still follow the paper menu system i.e whenever a customer enters a restaurant he is provided with a paper menu. The problem that lies in the traditional paper menu system is that whenever the menu list is updated, new printouts have to be taken off the menu.

This leads to violation of nature since there is unnecessary wastage of paper. Better way to tackle this situation could be scanning a QR code and an updated menu list appears.
